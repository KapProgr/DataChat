# DataChat - Startup Brief

## One-Liner
AI copilot for spreadsheet analysis that turns plain-English questions into instant insights and charts.

## Problem
Teams spend too much time in spreadsheets:
- manual formulas
- repetitive pivot tables
- slow ad-hoc reporting
- limited data literacy across non-technical roles

## Solution
DataChat provides:
- CSV/XLSX upload
- natural language analytics queries
- auto-generated charts
- instant exports (CSV/PNG)
- usage-based SaaS plans (Free + Pro)

## Target Users
- SMB founders and operators
- sales and finance teams
- marketing analysts
- freelancers and agencies handling client reports

## Why Now
- LLMs make natural-language analytics accessible.
- Teams need faster decisions with lean headcount.
- Spreadsheet-heavy workflows remain a major bottleneck.

## Business Model
- Free Tier: daily usage limits
- Pro Tier: monthly subscription with unlimited usage and priority support
- Future: team plan, seat-based pricing, API access

## Go-To-Market (MVP)
- founder-led outreach to SMBs
- demo-driven onboarding
- content around "replace spreadsheet busywork"
- partner channels: agencies and consultants

## Key Product Metrics
- activation: time to first successful query
- retention: weekly active analyzers
- quality: successful query rate
- conversion: free-to-pro upgrade rate
- value: reports generated per active user

## Current Stage
Commercial MVP with core SaaS loops implemented:
- authentication
- billing flow
- core analysis pipeline
- history and usage controls

## Next Milestones
1. Security hardening (auth enforcement, execution isolation)
2. Reliability and observability (rate limits, monitoring, retries)
3. Product depth (saved dashboards, scheduled reports, team workspace)
4. Growth loops (templates, onboarding automations, referral hooks)
